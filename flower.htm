Flower Island

Looking up the Calaguas Island map, you will discover that it is a group of islands. The most prominent beach where most tourists flock and which is generally called “Calaguas” is actually the “Mahabang Buhangin” situated in the Tinaga Island, one of the main islands of Calaguas. Going here takes a lot of time and effort, nonetheless, the whole experience is priceless.


How to get there?

Fly direct from Manila to Flower Island Resort by seaplane (1.5hours) or helicopter (2.0hours)

Departure times may vary depending on arrival of guests at the pier. Standard departure time is from 8:00AM-3:00PM.

Fly from Manila to El Nido via Air Swift flight

  • El Nido to Sibaltan Pier on private van: ₱3,000, one-way (1-4people max. capacity). Travel time, 1hour 15mins.++
  • From Sibaltan Pier to Flower Island by speedboat: ₱3,000, one way (1-4 people max. capacity). Travel time: 40mins.++
Departure times may vary depending on arrival of guests at the pier. Standard departure time is from 8:00AM-3:00PM.

Fly from Manila to Puerto Princesa (via local AirLines)
  • Option 1
    Puerto Princesa to Taytay terminal by commercial trip: ₱500/person (*approximate only), one-way via commercial van. Travel time, 4hours ++
    From Taytay terminal guests can take a tricycle going to the pier (5-10mins. travel)
  • Option 2
    Puerto Princesa to Taytay Pier by private van: ₱6,500, one-way (1-8people max. capacity). Travel time, 3hours ++.
    • From Taytay Pier to Flower Island by speedboat: Php 4,000.00, one-way (1-4 people max. capacity). Travel time, 1hour ++.
    • Departure times may vary depending on arrival of guests at the pier. Standard departure time is from 8:00AM-3:00PM.

Where to stay?

There is only one place you can stay in here in Flower Island and that is the Flower Island Resort

Flower Island Resort boasts its 250sqm air-conditioned tropical haven suite created by a famous French designer Chris Tardieu. It is the property's finest luxury bungalow to date.


What to do?

  • Exploring the Island
    For over three decades we have been protecting the flora and fauna surrounding the island to create a resort that is off the beaten path in harmonic balance with nature. Untangle your mind and leave the world far behind as you focus and heal amidst Flower Island Resort’s peaceful atmosphere while you explore the island

  • Picnic
    Enjoy a short speedboat ride to a nearby island, and experience a relaxing day out on the beach as we organize a private island picnic with an exotic spread of Filipino favorites such as pearl meat bbq, grilled seafood, to name a few.

  • Paddle boarding
    Fitness on the water! Sit or stand or do yoga on our paddle boards as you enjoy the ocean.

  • Kayaking
    Kayak at sea with solo, or with your loved ones.

  • The Peak
    For a breathtaking view, a trip onto the top of The Peak is a must. Guests can enjoy this unique vantage point where they can see the entire island while savoring the fresh air. A magical night under the stars in tower suites can also be arranged upon request.

  • Scuba diving
    Discover Flower Island and its nearby islands like Elephant Island, Casian Island, Maonsonon Island to name a few all of which have various marine life species and landscapes to discover.

    Palawan is known to be the last ecological frontier in the world. Discover multi-colored fish, corals, and swim with sea turtles.

  • Island Hopping
    Each one of these islands has its unique beauty for snorkelers and divers, even for those who just love to go for a boat ride and indulge in the scenic view surrounding Flower Island Resort.

  • Trekking
    Trek around the island or hike up to the Peak for a 360 view of Flower Island and its neighboring islands. The Peak is highly recommended for sunrise and/or sunset hikes. We also have rooms available up at the tower!

  • Pearl Farm Tour
    Take part in a unique experience as you visit a working pearl farm and learn how a pearl, our National Gem, is born.
